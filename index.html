About Me

Hello! I’m Ritabrata Bose from India. I’m currently pursuing my Masters degree (M.Sc.) in Economics from University of Calcutta, India. I have completed my Undergraduate degree (B.Sc.) in Economics (honours) with Mathematics & Statistics as general papers from University of Calcutta, India.
Currently I am working on two projects - one, concerning the effect of globalization on the Indian middleclass society (group project) (under Prof. Sudakshina Gupta) and another one on the sectoral growth performance of Indian states (under Prof. Panchanan Das).

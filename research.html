Slums in Kolkata: A Socio-Economic Analysis (with S. Ghosh), The Empirical Econometrics and Quantitative Economics Letters, Vol 4 No 4 (December 2015)

Poverty is a major curse to the society. Slums are the face of urban poverty and illiteracy. This paper presents and discusses primary data from a survey of 96 households taken from five different slums of Kolkata. It attempts to examine the socio-economic aspects of slums surveyed in this paper in terms of income, education, savings, sanitation, financial inclusion etc... More
